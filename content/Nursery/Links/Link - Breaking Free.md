**Link: [Breaking Free](https://www.forbrukerradet.no/breakingfree) and [Report PDF](https://storage02.forbrukerradet.no/media/2026/02/breaking-free-pathways-to-a-fair-technological-future.pdf)**

A research study and report done by the Norwegian Consumer Council about enshittification and how to resist it. The report describes techniques and tactics used by big tech companies such as Google, Apple, Facebook, Amazon, and Microsoft to retain both consumer and business customers while also degrading the quality of the services they provide.
