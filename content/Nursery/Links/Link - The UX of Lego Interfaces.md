**Link: [The UX of Lego Interfaces](https://interactionmagic.com/UX-LEGO-Interfaces)**

An exploration of how to design good interfaces using Legos.

Inputs can be _differentiated_ by:
- size
- shape
- color
- texture
- position
- operation

It is also important to effectively _organize_ inputs. Different methods of organization enable different kinds of tasks. Interfaces could be organized by:
- feature
- operation
- technology
- use-case
